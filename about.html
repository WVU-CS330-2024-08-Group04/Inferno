About

Inferno: Wildfire Control & Tracking is an application that uses data from National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration(NOAA) to display active fires as well as predict areas that could have wildfires.The data used in the applicaton from NOAA consists of the Global Forecast System(GFS) and the Joint Polar Satellite System(JPSS).
